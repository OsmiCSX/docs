## Flex
Utilities for controlling how flex items both grow and shrink and also available for full width.
Namespace | Output
--------- | ------
**full** | `{ width: '100%' }`
**flex** | `{ flex: 1 }`
**flex-0** | `{ flex: 0 }`
**flex-2** | `{ flex: 2 }`
**flex-3** | `{ flex: 3 }`
**flex-4** | `{ flex: 4 }`
**flex-5** | `{ flex: 5 }`
**flex-6** | `{ flex: 6 }`
**flex-7** | `{ flex: 7 }`
**flex-8** | `{ flex: 8 }`
**flex-9** | `{ flex: 9 }`
**flex-10** | `{ flex: 10 }`
**flex-11** | `{ flex: 11 }`
**flex-12** | `{ flex: 12 }`

## Flex Direction

Namespace | Output
--------- | ------
